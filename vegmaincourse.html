Paneer Masala

Ingredients

  • 200 g paneer cubes
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt

How to make Paneer Masala

  1. Sauté onion in oil.
  2. Add ginger garlic paste.
  3. Add tomato puree and spices.
  4. Cook for 10 minutes.
  5. Add paneer cubes.
  6. Cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Serve hot.

Chhole Masala

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chickpeas
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tsp chhole masala
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • Salt

How to make Chhole Masala

  1. Cook soaked chickpeas.
  2. Sauté onion and garlic.
  3. Add tomato puree.
  4. Add spices.
  5. Add chickpeas.
  6. Cook for 10 minutes.
  7. Serve hot.

Paneer Do Pyaza

Ingredients

  • 200 g paneer
  • 2 onions (chopped)
  • 1 onion (cubed)
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • Spices and salt

How to make Paneer Do Pyaza

  1. Sauté chopped onions.
  2. Add ginger garlic paste.
  3. Add tomato puree.
  4. Add spices.
  5. Add paneer and onion cubes.
  6. Cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Serve hot.

Butter Paneer

Ingredients

  • 200 g paneer
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • Spices and salt

How to make Butter Paneer

  1. Sauté ginger garlic in butter.
  2. Add tomato puree.
  3. Add spices.
  4. Add paneer cubes.
  5. Add cream.
  6. Cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Serve hot.

Mushroom Masala

Ingredients

  • 200 g mushrooms
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt

How to make Mushroom Masala

  1. Sauté onion and garlic.
  2. Add tomato puree.
  3. Add spices.
  4. Add mushrooms.
  5. Cook for 8 minutes.
  6. Simmer for 3 minutes.
  7. Serve hot.

Mix Veg

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • Salt

How to make Mix Veg

  1. Sauté onion and garlic.
  2. Add tomato puree.
  3. Add spices.
  4. Add mixed vegetables.
  5. Cook for 10 minutes.
  6. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  7. Serve hot.
