Butter Chicken

Ingredients

  • 300 g chicken pieces
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • Salt and spices

How to make Butter Chicken

  1. Sauté onion in butter.
  2. Add ginger garlic paste.
  3. Add tomato puree and spices.
  4. Add chicken pieces.
  5. Cook for 15 minutes.
  6. Add cream and simmer.
  7. Serve hot.

Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • 300 g chicken
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt

How to make Chicken Curry

  1. Sauté onion and garlic.
  2. Add tomato puree.
  3. Add spices.
  4. Add chicken pieces.
  5. Cook for 20 minutes.
  6. Add water if needed.
  7. Serve hot.

Mutton Curry

Ingredients

  • 300 g mutton
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • Salt

How to make Mutton Curry

  1. Sauté onion and garlic.
  2. Add tomato puree.
  3. Add spices.
  4. Add mutton pieces.
  5. Cook for 30 minutes.
  6. Simmer until tender.
  7. Serve hot.

Egg Curry

Ingredients

  • 4 boiled eggs
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • Salt

How to make Egg Curry

  1. Sauté onion and garlic.
  2. Add tomato puree.
  3. Add spices.
  4. Add boiled eggs.
  5. Cook for 10 minutes.
  6. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  7. Serve hot.
