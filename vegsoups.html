Tomato Soup

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe tomatoes
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 tsp garlic
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • Salt to taste

How to make Tomato Soup

  1. Boil tomatoes with water.
  2. Blend tomatoes into smooth puree.
  3. Sauté onion and garlic in butter.
  4. Add tomato puree.
  5. Add salt and pepper.
  6. Cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Serve hot.

Veg Manchow Soup

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup cabbage
  • 1/4 cup carrot
  • 1/4 cup capsicum
  • 2 cups vegetable stock
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp chili sauce
  • 1 tbsp cornflour slurry
  • Salt and pepper

How to make Manchow Soup

  1. Sauté garlic in oil.
  2. Add cabbage, carrot and capsicum.
  3. Add vegetable stock.
  4. Add soy and chili sauce.
  5. Add cornflour slurry.
  6. Cook until thick.
  7. Serve hot with fried noodles.

Sweet Corn Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sweet corn
  • 3 cups vegetable stock
  • 1 tbsp cornflour
  • 1/4 cup carrot
  • 1/4 cup beans
  • Salt to taste
  • White pepper

How to make Sweet Corn Soup

  1. Boil vegetable stock.
  2. Add corn and vegetables.
  3. Add cornflour slurry.
  4. Cook for 3 minutes.
  5. Add salt and pepper.
  6. Simmer for 2 minutes.
  7. Serve hot.

Hot and Sour Veg Soup

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup carrot
  • 1/4 cup cabbage
  • 3 cups vegetable stock
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • 1 tbsp cornflour slurry
  • Salt and pepper

How to make Hot&Sour Soup

  1. Boil vegetable stock.
  2. Add vegetables.
  3. Add soy sauce and vinegar.
  4. Add cornflour slurry.
  5. Cook for 3–4 minutes.
  6. Add salt and pepper.
  7. Serve hot.

Mixed Vegetable Soup

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup carrot
  • 1/4 cup beans
  • 1/4 cup peas
  • 3 cups vegetable stock
  • 1 tsp garlic
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • Salt

How to make Vegetable Soup

  1. Sauté garlic in oil.
  2. Add chopped vegetables.
  3. Add vegetable stock.
  4. Cook for 10 minutes.
  5. Add salt and pepper.
  6. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  7. Serve hot.

Vegetable Clear Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 carrot
  • 1/4 cup cabbage
  • 1/4 cup beans
  • 3 cups water
  • 1 tsp garlic
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

How to make Clear Soup

  1. Boil water.
  2. Add vegetables and garlic.
  3. Cook for 15 minutes.
  4. Add salt and pepper.
  5. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Strain if needed.
  7. Serve hot.
