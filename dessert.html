Chocolate Lava Cake

Ingredients

  • 100 g dark chocolate
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • Vanilla essence
  • Salt

How to make Chocolate Lava Cake

  1. Melt chocolate and butter.
  2. Mix egg and sugar.
  3. Add flour and vanilla.
  4. Pour into moulds.
  5. Bake for 10 minutes.
  6. Cool slightly.
  7. Serve warm.

Tiramisu

Ingredients

  • 10 ladyfinger biscuits
  • 1 cup cream
  • 1/2 cup coffee
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • Cocoa powder
  • Vanilla essence
  • Chocolate flakes

How to make Tiramisu

  1. Dip biscuits in coffee.
  2. Layer biscuits in tray.
  3. Add whipped cream.
  4. Repeat the layers.
  5. Dust cocoa powder.
  6. Chill for 2 hours.
  7. Serve cold.

Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cream cheese
  • 1 cup biscuits
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • Vanilla essence
  • Fruit topping

How to make Cheesecake

  1. Crush biscuits finely.
  2. Mix with melted butter.
  3. Prepare cream cheese mix.
  4. Spread over biscuit base.
  5. Chill for 3 hours.
  6. Add fruit topping.
  7. Serve cold.

Brownie

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • Chocolate chips
  • Vanilla essence

How to make Brownie

  1. Mix butter and sugar.
  2. Add egg and vanilla.
  3. Add flour and cocoa.
  4. Mix chocolate chips.
  5. Pour into tray.
  6. Bake for 20 minutes.
  7. Serve warm.

Panna Cotta

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cream
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp gelatin
  • Vanilla essence
  • Berry sauce
  • Mint leaves

How to make Panna Cotta

  1. Heat milk and cream.
  2. Add sugar and vanilla.
  3. Mix gelatin well.
  4. Pour into moulds.
  5. Chill for 3 hours.
  6. Add berry sauce.
  7. Serve cold.

Vanilla Ice Cream

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 cup cream
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • Vanilla essence
  • Ice cubes
  • Chocolate syrup
  • Nuts

How to make Vanilla Ice Cream

  1. Mix milk and cream.
  2. Add sugar and vanilla.
  3. Blend until smooth.
  4. Freeze for 5 hours.
  5. Stir once in between.
  6. Add toppings.
  7. Serve chilled.
