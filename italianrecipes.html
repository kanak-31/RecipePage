Pasta Pomodoro

Ingredients

  • 200 g pasta
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Italian herbs
  • Chili flakes
  • Salt

How to make Pasta Pomodoro

  1. Boil the pasta.
  2. Sauté garlic in olive oil.
  3. Add tomato puree.
  4. Add herbs and spices.
  5. Mix pasta well.
  6. Cook for 3 minutes.
  7. Serve hot.

Margherita Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza base
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • Tomato slices
  • Olive oil
  • Italian herbs
  • Salt

How to make Margherita Pizza

  1. Spread sauce on pizza base.
  2. Add cheese and tomatoes.
  3. Sprinkle herbs.
  4. Bake for 12 minutes.
  5. Drizzle olive oil.
  6. Slice the pizza.
  7. Serve hot.

Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 6 lasagna sheets
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup cheese
  • 1 onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • Italian herbs
  • Salt

How to make Lasagna

  1. Boil lasagna sheets.
  2. Cook onion and garlic.
  3. Add tomato sauce.
  4. Layer sheets and sauce.
  5. Add cheese on top.
  6. Bake for 20 minutes.
  7. Serve hot.

Risotto

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1 cup vegetable stock
  • Italian herbs
  • Salt

How to make Risotto

  1. Sauté onion in butter.
  2. Add rice and cook.
  3. Pour vegetable stock.
  4. Add cream and herbs.
  5. Cook until creamy.
  6. Stir well.
  7. Serve hot.

Bruschetta

Ingredients

  • 4 bread slices
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • Olive oil
  • Basil leaves
  • Chili flakes
  • Salt

How to make Bruschetta

  1. Toast the bread slices.
  2. Mix tomatoes and garlic.
  3. Add olive oil and herbs.
  4. Spread mixture on bread.
  5. Sprinkle chili flakes.
  6. Garnish with basil.
  7. Serve fresh.

Alfredo Pasta

Ingredients

  • 200 g pasta
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 cup cheese
  • Italian herbs
  • Salt

How to make Alfredo Pasta

  1. Boil the pasta.
  2. Melt butter in pan.
  3. Add garlic and cream.
  4. Add cheese and herbs.
  5. Mix pasta well.
  6. Cook for 2 minutes.
  7. Serve hot.
