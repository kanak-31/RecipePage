• Jeera Rice
  • Veg Pulao
  • Lemon Rice

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds (jeera)
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 cloves
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander (optional)

Jeera Rice

  1. Wash basmati rice and soak for 15 minutes.
  2. Heat ghee in a pan.
  3. Add cumin seeds, bay leaf and cloves.
  4. Let them crackle for a few seconds.
  5. Add soaked rice and sauté for 1 minute.
  6. Add water and salt.
  7. Cover and cook until rice becomes soft.
  8. Garnish with coriander and serve hot.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 small onion
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste

Veg Pulao

  1. Wash basmati rice and soak for 15 minutes.
  2. Heat oil or ghee in a pan or cooker.
  3. Add cumin seeds and bay leaf.
  4. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden.
  5. Add mixed vegetables and cook for 2–3 minutes.
  6. Add soaked rice and stir gently.
  7. Add water, salt and garam masala.
  8. Cover and cook until the rice is soft and fluffy.
  9. Garnish with coriander and serve hot.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 8–10 curry leaves
  • 2 green chilies (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste

Lemon Rice

  1. Wash basmati rice and cook it with water.
  2. Let the cooked rice cool slightly.
  3. Heat oil in a pan.
  4. Add mustard seeds and let them crackle.
  5. Add curry leaves and chopped green chilies.
  6. Sauté for a few seconds.
  7. Add the cooked rice and mix well.
  8. Add lemon juice and salt, then serve.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 tsp oil
  • Extra flour for rolling
  • Butter (optional)
  • Ghee (optional)
  • Dry flour for dusting

Roti

  1. Take wheat flour in a bowl.
  2. Add salt and mix well.
  3. Gradually add water and knead soft dough.
  4. Rest the dough for 10 minutes.
  5. Divide dough into small balls.
  6. Roll each ball into a thin circle.
  7. Cook on a hot tawa until both sides brown.
  8. Apply butter or ghee and serve.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Water as needed
  • Butter for brushing

Naan

  1. Mix flour, sugar, salt and baking powder.
  2. Add yogurt and oil.
  3. Add water and knead soft dough.
  4. Cover and rest for 1 hour.
  5. Divide dough into small balls.
  6. Roll each ball into oval shape.
  7. Cook on hot tawa until golden spots appear.
  8. Brush with butter and serve.

Ingredients

  • 1 bread loaf
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tbsp chopped parsley
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 pinch black pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Grated cheese (optional)

Garlic Bread

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix butter, garlic and parsley.
  3. Add salt and black pepper.
  4. Slice the bread loaf.
  5. Spread garlic butter on each slice.
  6. Drizzle a little olive oil.
  7. Bake for 8–10 minutes until crispy.
  8. Add cheese if desired and serve.

Fruit Salad

  1. Wash all fruits properly.
  2. Chop apple and papaya into small cubes.
  3. Slice the banana.
  4. Add grapes and pomegranate seeds.
  5. Put all fruits into a large bowl.
  6. Add honey and lemon juice.
  7. Mix gently so fruits don’t break.
  8. Garnish with mint leaves and serve.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice

  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1 tomato (chopped)

  • 1 tsp biryani masala

  • Salt to taste

Chicken Biryani

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice for 20 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and fry sliced onions.
  3. Add ginger garlic paste and sauté.
  4. Add chicken pieces and cook for a few minutes.
  5. Add tomatoes, yogurt and biryani masala.
  6. Cook until chicken is half done.
  7. Add soaked rice and required water.
  8. Cook covered until rice is soft and serve.

Ingredients

  • 250 g boneless chicken
  • 1/2 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Chicken Pakora

  1. Wash and cut chicken into small pieces.
  2. Take chicken in a bowl.
  3. Add ginger garlic paste and spices.
  4. Add gram flour and salt.
  5. Mix everything well.
  6. Heat oil in a pan.
  7. Drop small portions of the mixture into hot oil.
  8. Fry until golden brown and crispy.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp garam masala

Chicken Tikka

  1. Wash chicken pieces properly.

  3. Add ginger garlic paste and spices.
  4. Add lemon juice and salt.
  5. Mix well to make marinade.
  6. Add chicken pieces and coat well.
  7. Marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  8. Grill or cook on pan until done.

Egg Roll

Ingredients

  • 250 g boneless chicken
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp red chili sauce
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 capsicum (chopped)
  • Salt to taste

Chicken Manchurian

  1. Cut chicken into small pieces.
  2. Mix chicken with cornflour and salt.
  3. Deep fry chicken until golden.
  4. Heat oil in another pan.
  5. Add ginger garlic paste and sauté.
  6. Add onion and capsicum.
  7. Add soy sauce and chili sauce.
  8. Add fried chicken and mix well.

Ingredients

  • Salt to taste

Chicken Fried Rice

  1. Heat oil in a wok.

  3. Add chicken pieces and cook well.
  4. Add chopped vegetables.
  5. Stir fry for 2–3 minutes.
  6. Add cooked rice.
  7. Add soy sauce, pepper and salt.
  8. Mix well and serve hot.
