Paneer Tikka

Ingredients

  • 200 g paneer (cubed)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt (curd)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Onion and capsicum cubes

How to make Paneer Tikka

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, lemon juice, oil, and salt.
  2. Add paneer cubes, onion, and capsicum to the marinade and mix well.
  3. Cover and marinate for about 30 minutes.
  4. Thread the paneer and vegetables onto skewers.
  5. Grill in an oven or cook in a pan until lightly charred and cooked.
  6. Serve hot with mint chutney and lemon wedges.

Spring Roll

Ingredients

  • 10 spring roll wrappers
  • 1 cup cabbage (finely shredded)
  • 1/2 cup carrot (grated)
  • 1/2 cup capsicum (thinly sliced)
  • 1 tsp garlic (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil (for cooking filling)
  • Oil for deep frying

How to make Spring Roll

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté chopped garlic for a few seconds.
  2. Add cabbage, carrot, and capsicum. Stir-fry for 2–3 minutes.
  3. Add soy sauce, black pepper, and salt. Mix well and cook for another minute.
  4. Let the filling cool slightly.
  5. Place some filling on a spring roll wrapper, fold the sides, and roll it tightly.
  6. Seal the edge with a little water or flour paste.
  7. Heat oil in a pan and deep fry the rolls until golden brown and crispy.
  8. Serve hot with chili sauce or ketchup.

Aloo Tikki

Ingredients

  • 3 boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • 1/4 cup peas (boiled)
  • 1 tsp ginger (grated)
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp breadcrumbs
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil (for shallow frying)

How to make Aloo Tikki

  1. In a bowl, mix mashed potatoes and boiled peas.
  2. Add ginger, red chili powder, garam masala, breadcrumbs, and salt.
  3. Mix everything well to form a dough-like mixture.
  4. Divide the mixture and shape into small round patties.
  5. Heat oil in a pan and place the patties on it.
  6. Shallow fry until both sides become golden brown and crispy.
  7. Serve hot with green chutney or tomato ketchup.

Cheese Balls

Ingredients

  • 1 cup boiled sweet corn
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 2 tbsp breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

How to make Cheese Balls

  1. In a bowl, mix boiled corn, grated cheese, cornflour, breadcrumbs, pepper, chili flakes, and salt.
  2. Mix well until it forms a soft mixture.
  3. Take small portions and shape them into round balls.
  4. Heat oil in a pan for deep frying.
  5. Fry the balls until they turn golden brown and crispy.
  6. Remove and place on tissue paper to absorb excess oil.
  7. Serve hot with mayonnaise or chili sauce.

Veg Cutlet

Ingredients

  • 2 boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, peas, beans)
  • 1 tsp ginger (grated)
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp breadcrumbs
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for shallow frying

How to make Veg Cutlet

  1. Boil and mash the potatoes in a bowl.
  2. Add boiled mixed vegetables to the mashed potatoes.
  3. Add ginger, red chili powder, garam masala, breadcrumbs, and salt.
  4. Mix everything well to form a soft dough.
  5. Shape the mixture into small round or oval patties.
  6. Heat oil in a pan and place the cutlets on it.
  7. Shallow fry until both sides become golden brown and crispy.
  8. Serve hot with tomato ketchup or green chutney.

Chana Kabab

Ingredients

  • 1 cup boiled chickpeas (chana)
  • 1 boiled potato (mashed)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp breadcrumbs
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for shallow frying

How to make Paneer Tikka

  1. Mash the boiled chickpeas in a bowl.
  2. Add mashed potato, ginger-garlic paste, spices, breadcrumbs, coriander leaves, and salt.
  3. Mix everything well to form a soft mixture.
  4. Shape the mixture into small round or oval kababs.
  5. Heat oil in a pan and place the kababs on it.
  6. Shallow fry until both sides turn golden brown and crispy.
  7. Serve hot with green chutney or tomato ketchup.
